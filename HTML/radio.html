Lunnelle

An originator of the 3am kebab celebration shuffle, Lunnelle shot to stardom DJing in chip shops across Newcastle and Dublin before getting a record deal with XL records at 19. The Grammy nominated DJ from Hillsborough, Sheffield regularly collabs with artists from Santigold, Jay Rock and H.E.R.

Loki

The Trickster from Richter, Loki's identity has been hidden but the DJ from Montpellier, France has made nothing but sonic waves playing to sold out crowds producing some of the most exciting electro music during a global tour while releasing 4 albums in 4 years.

Symfoni

A Hacienda legend Symfoni has been praised for keeping the flame of Jungle and DnB alive for a new generation.

Wave Break

Wave Break's Boom Bap infused mixtapes garnered a near cult-like following and resulted in Drake, Migos and DJ Khaled jumping on remixes of his hit 'Mini Roll Mansion'. The self-styled 'Squire of Drip' is set to release his debut this summer.

George and Jar

Twins from Hackney spent 10 years in Shanghai spreading an ecclectic mix of Afrobeats, Folk and gems from World Music before returning to the UK to shock speakers and crowds with their unique blend of Audio Gaudium.

PNYWYZE

According to Pusha T PNYWYZE was 'busy earning stripes like a tiger's skin', certified in the streets and airwaves, The P has been able to clown opponents like his namesame, all while moving between platinum pop hits and bronx timbaland heavy street bangers like 'Lean Yo Jaw'.
